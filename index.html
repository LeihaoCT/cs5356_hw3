About Me

Hello! My name is Doudou. Nice to meet you! I'm a gray tabby with green eyes and a passion for windowsill contemplation. When I'm not monitoring Roosevelt Island from my high-rise perch, I enjoy the finer things in life: sunbeam naps, strategic placement of fur on dark clothing, and judging human activities from a distance. Originally from Philadelphia, I've now adapted myself to apartment life as a New York cat, graciously allowing my human companion to continue paying rent. My sophisticated demeanor masks my true nature - a mischievous soul who can't resist scratching the couch at 3 AM for cosmetic purposes.
